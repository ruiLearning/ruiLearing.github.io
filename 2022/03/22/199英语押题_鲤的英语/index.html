模版

综合押题使⽤说明

  1. 模板化。为了减轻学习负担，本材料中所选的6个话题，都采⽤同⼀套模板写出的。你可以将其视作是⼀个作⽂的保底⽅案。模板的句型均来⾃18炼。具体模板为：

If you look hard enough, you will find that【 】.Two reasons can bear this out. The leading cause is 【 】，and it can account for【 】. Secondly, when it comes to【 】，it is well known that【 】. And it can boil down to【 】. Without doubt【 】 will exert a far-reaching/undesirable/unimaginable/disastrous effect on【 】.

Judging by what is discussed above, it is not difficult to draw the conclusion that【 】.What is to be done? Pretty much everyone agrees on the prescription(way/method/solution/suggestion) that【 】.

  1. 如果你感到时间很紧，可以选择直接掌握该模板+本材料中的⽂章。
  2. 英⼀的同学重点看话题1、2、3、4，英⼆的同学重点看话题3、4、5、6。
  3. 有很多同学更喜欢我们课上所教的句⼦模板按需组合的⽅式，当然也没有问题。这时，你可以不使⽤本材料中的模板。
  4. 如果你时间⽐较宽裕，可以在看完本材料后再去看精准写作2-论据题库。

其他注意事项:

  1. 本材料中的⽂章为论述部分（第⼆+第三段），不含第⼀段。因为第⼀段是完全因具体题⽬⽽变的。
  2. 本材料中的⽂章为⼤作⽂部分。⼩作⽂部分不⽤额外准备押题，只需要将第16、17、18炼的笔记中的⼀些⽤词认真复习，且完成整篇专训中的⼩作⽂即可。因为从往年的经验来看，各⼤机构所给的押题材料，最后所谓的押中其实都是类型碰到，⽽不是题⽬押中。⽐如，某机构给出的押题材料中有⼀封道歉信，但具体题⽬可能是李明因为借书问题⽽向李红道歉，⽽考试可能考的是李明因为迟到⽽向⽼师道歉，在这种情况下，各⼤机构依旧会说是押中了题。押中了什么呢？道歉信。但实际上，内容细节截然不同。因此，⼩作⽂部分不建议你做具体题⽬细节层⾯的押题，⽽是将第16、17、18炼的笔记中的⼀些⽤词认真复习（⽐如说点实的、说点虚的），且完成整篇专训中的⼩作⽂即可。

话题1：⾃律【英⼀需重视】

中⽂：
如果你观察的⾜够仔细，会发现⾃律对我们的⽣活有深远影响。两点原因可以解释。⾸要原因是⾃律的⼈更容易养成好习惯，⽽这能解释为什么⾃律的⼈会更加⾃信。其次，当提及⾃律时，众所周知的是⾃律的⼈会更容易成功。⽽这可以归结于⼀种意识——由⾃律带来的管理⾃⼰⾏为的意识。毫⽆疑问，⾃律的习惯将对每个⼈的⼈⽣产⽣积极的影响。
根据以上所讨论的来判断，不难得出结论：我们应该养成⾃律的习惯。应该怎么办呢？ 很多⼈都赞成这⼀⽅案：努⼒找到⾃⼰热爱的事情去做是⾃律最有效的⽅法之⼀。

英⽂：
If you look hard enough, you will find that self-discipline has a far-reaching impact on our
lives. Two reasons can bear this out. The leading cause is that disciplined people are more
likely to form good habits, and it can account for why such people are more confident.
Secondly, when it comes to self-discipline, it is well known that people with this trait are
more prone to succeed. And it can boil down to a kind of consciousness —— managing one’s
own deeds brought about by self-discipline. Without doubt, this habit will exert a positive
effect on everyone’s life.
Judging by what is discussed above, it is not difficult to draw the conclusion that we
should develop the habit of self-discipline. What is to be done? Pretty much everyone agrees
on the prescription that trying to find where your passion lies is one of the most effective
ways of self-discipline.

话题3：终身学习【英⼀英⼆都可重视】

中⽂：
如果你观察的⾜够仔细，会发现终身学习对我们的⼯作和⽣活有深远影响。
两点原因可以解释。
⾸要原因是未来的职业竞争将越来越激烈，⽽这能解释为什么只有持续学习的⼈才有更好的职业发展前景。
其次，当提及终身学习时，众所周知的是只有坚持终身学习的⼈才能在未来战胜⼀个⼜⼀个全新的挑战。
⽽这可以归结于快速更新的知识。毫⽆疑问，这将对⼈们的学习观念产⽣积极的影 响。
根据以上所讨论的来判断，不难得出结论：我们应该重视终身学习。
应该怎么办呢？
很多⼈都赞成这⼀⽅案：我们需要在各⼤媒体上加⼤对终身学习益处的宣传。
英⽂：
If you look hard enough, you will find that lifelong learning has a far-reaching impact on our work and life.
Two reasons can bear this out.
The leading cause is that the future career competition will be more and more fierce, and it can account for why only those who keep learning will have a better career development prospect.
Secondly, when it comes to lifelong learning，it is well known that only those who stick to learning can overcome one brand-new challenge after another in the future.
And it can boil down to rapidly updated knowledge.
Without doubt, this will exert a positive effect on people’s concept of learning.
Judging by what is discussed above, it is not difficult to draw the conclusion that we should attach importance to lifelong learning.
What is to be done? Pretty much everyone agrees on the prescription that we need to promote the benefits of lifelong learning in all major media.

话题4：⽓候变暖【英⼀英⼆都可重视】

暖时，众所周知的是全球⽓候变化可能导致出现极端天⽓的频率增加。⽽这可以归结于过量的碳排
放。毫⽆疑问，这将对全球⽓候产⽣灾难性的影响。

话题5：交通拥堵【英⼆需重视】

中⽂：
如果你观察的⾜够仔细，会发现交通拥堵对我们的⽇常⽣活有深远影响。
两点原因可以解释。
⾸要原因是随着⽣活⽔平的上升越来越多⼈买得起私家⻋，⽽这能解释为什么路上的⻋越来越多。
其次，当提及交通拥堵时，众所周知的是公共交通不够便利也是个重要的影响因素。
⽽这可以归结于城市的经济和管理⽔平。 毫⽆疑问，这将对城市交通产⽣不利的影响。
根据以上所讨论的来判断，不难得出结论：我们应该想办法缓解交通拥堵。
应该怎么办呢？ 很多⼈都赞成这⼀⽅案：我们应该⼤⼒发展智能交通，提⾼城市的交通管理⽔平。
英⽂：
If you look hard enough, you will find that traffic congestion has a far-reaching impact on our daily life.
Two reasons can bear this out.
The leading cause is that more and more people can afford private cars with the rise of living standards, and it can account for why there are more and more cars on the road.
Secondly, when it comes to traffic jams, it is well known that the inconvenience of public transport is also an important factor.
And it can boil down to the economic and management level of the city. Without doubt, this will exert an undesirable effect on the traffic in the city.
Judging by what is discussed above, it is not difficult to draw the conclusion that we should find ways to alleviate traffic congestion.
What is to be done? Pretty much everyone agrees on the prescription that we should develop intelligent transportation vigorously and improve the level of urban traffic management.

话题6：健康饮⻝【英⼆需重视】

中⽂：
如果你观察的⾜够仔细，会发现健康饮⻝对我们的⼈⽣有深远影响。

⾸要原因是它可以让我们有更好的精⼒，⽽这能解释为什么精英⼈群更重视健康饮⻝。
其次，当提及健康饮⻝时，众所周知的是我们应该减少脂肪和糖类的摄⼊。
⽽这可以归结于那些由不健康的饮⻝⽅式导致的肥胖问题。毫⽆疑问，这将对个⼈健康产⽣不利的影响。
根据以上所讨论的来判断，不难得出结论：我们应该重视健康饮⻝。
应该怎么办呢？ 很多⼈都赞成这⼀⽅案：我们应当控制每顿饭的分量，不能暴饮暴⻝，多吃⽔果蔬菜以及⻥类。
英⽂：
If you look hard enough, you will find that a healthy diet has a profound impact on our lives.
Two reasons can bear this out.
The leading cause is that such a diet can make us have better energy，and it can account for why the elite attach more importance to healthy eating.
Secondly, when it comes to healthy eating，it is well known that we should reduce the intake of fats and sugars.
And it can boil down to obesity caused by unhealthy diets.
Without doubt, this will exert an adverse effect on personal health.
Judging by what is discussed above, it is not difficult to draw the conclusion that we should lay emphasis on healthy eating.
What is to be done? Pretty much everyone agrees on the prescription that we should control the amount of each meal and do not eat and drink too much.

如果你观察的⾜够仔细，会发现健康饮⻝对我们的⼈⽣有深远影响。
两点原因可以解释。
⾸要原因是它可以让我们有更好的精⼒，⽽这能解释为什么精英⼈群更重视健康饮⻝。
其次，当提及健康饮⻝时，众所周知的是我们应该减少脂肪和糖类的摄⼊。
⽽这可以归结于那些由不健康的饮⻝⽅式导致的肥胖问题。
毫⽆疑问，这将对个⼈健康产⽣不利的影响。
根据以上所讨论的来判断，不难得出结论：我们应该重视健康饮⻝。应该怎么办呢？
很多⼈都赞成这⼀⽅案：我们应当控制每顿饭的分量，不能暴饮暴⻝，多吃⽔果蔬菜以及⻥类。

If you look hard enough, you will find that a healthy diet has a profound impact on our lives.
The leading cause is that such a diet can make us have a better energy, and it can account for why the elite attach more importance to healthy eating.
Secondly, when it comes to healthy eating, it is well known that we should reduce the intake of fats and sugars.
And it can boil down to obesity caused by unhealthy diets.
Without doubt, this will exert an adverse effect on personal health.
Judging by what is discussed above, it is not difficult to draw the conclusion that we should lay emphasis on healthy eating.
What is to be done? Pretty much everyone agrees on the prescription that we should control the amount of each meal and do not eat and drink too much.
