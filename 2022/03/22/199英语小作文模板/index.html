199英语小作文模板

发表于2022-03-22|更新于2022-09-23|英语
|阅读量:

小作文
目的
I am writing this letter/email to
通知：inform you that xx
感谢：thank you for xx
投诉：complain about xx
推荐： offer some suggestions about xx
道歉：apologize to you for xx
邀请：invite you to xx
祝贺：congratulations to you on xx
咨询：inquire about xx
建议：recommend to you xx
介绍：introduce to you xx
通知：
感谢：
投诉：
建议：
道歉：
邀请：
祝贺：
咨询：
推荐：
介绍：
回信
我很高兴知道xx。祝贺你!
I am very glad to know that xx.Congratulations!
基于你的上一封来信/要求/问题，我在此主题(例:给你推荐一本书)。
With reference to your last email/request/question, I am writing to 主题 (e.g. recommend to you a book).
结尾
投诉、邀请、咨询
我期待着您在方便的时候尽快给我答复。提前感谢您的时间和关注。
I am looking forward to a reply at your earliest convenience.Thank you in advance for your time and attention.
感谢
Thank you again for xx
祝贺
Congratulation again for xx
抱歉
我再次为所发生的事情道歉。我希望没有造成太大的不便。
I apologize again for what has happened.I do hope that it has not caused too much inconvenience.
建议、推荐、通知
我希望我的推荐对你有用。如果你有任何问题，请随时与我联系。我很乐意与您详细讨论此事。
I do hope that you will find my recommendation useful.If you have any questions, please feel free to let me know,and I will be happy to discuss this matter with you in greater detail.
结尾凑字
Wish you and your family all the best!
祝您和您的家人万事如意!
As year 2022 is around the corner, I do wish you and your family a fulfilling and happy new year ahead.
在2022年即将到来之际，我衷心祝愿你和你的家人有一个充实和快乐的新年。

感谢信
要注意用语礼貌真诚。
第一段：表明写信的目的
第二段：说明感谢原因
第三段：再次表明感谢
But your timely encouragement calmed me down and filled me with enough confidence.
但是您及时的鼓励让我平静下来并有了充分的自信。
I am writing this letter to express my genuine gratitude for your kind help.
我写此信的目的是为了表达我对您的诚挚的感谢。
I am writing to express my sincere thankfulness to you for your…(感谢的具体的内容：名词)
我来写信向您的…致以衷心的感谢。
I would like to thank you from the bottom of my heart for… (感谢的具体的内容：名词)
我衷心的感谢您…
Please accept my profound appreciation for your…(感谢的具体的内容：名词)
请接收我对您…的忠心的谢意
With deepest gratitude I write you here a few lines.
怀着感激之情我为您写下了这几句话。
My gratitude to you is beyond the power of words.
我对您的感激之情无以言表。
Words fail to convey my gratitude to you.
我对您的感激之情无以言表。
I feel obliged to thank you again.
我必须对您再次表示感谢。
I am writing the letter to express my sincere thanks to you for your help.
我写此信是要对您的帮助表达我由衷的感谢。
I am writing to convey my heartfelt gratitude to you for your kind…
我写这封信是想表达我对您…的衷心感谢。
Thanks again for your … to me.
再次感谢您…

建议信
建议信就是向他人或机构提出自己的建议的文体，所以观点要合乎情理，语气不能太强硬，注意礼貌用语。
第一段：表明写信目的或者依据
第二段：提出自己的具体建议
第三段：提出期望并礼貌性的结束全文
And it’s great pleasure to give me some advice on how to …
我很高兴能就如何…向您提出我的一些建议。
Above all,place great emphasis on the …(建议内容1)，for it it a precondition and basis for …(视主题而定)
最重要的是…，高度重视…，因为它是…一个先决条件和基础。
In addition, it’s necessary to have an excellent command of …(建议内容2)，which is vitally import to …(视主题而定)
此外，精通…是有必要的，因为这对…至关重要。
And lastly,it is must for you to …(建议内容3)，considering that…(视主题而定)
最后，考虑到…，你必须…
Wish you greater advances in …(视主题而定)
祝你在…取的更大的进步。
I am writing this letter for the purpose of conveying my deepest concern about …
我写此信的目的是为了表达我对…的深切关注。
It must be pointed out that the current situation is going form bad to worse.
必须指出当前的情形正在越变越糟。
Therefore,it is imperative for us to take some drastic measures.
因此，我们急需采取一些有力的措施。
In addition,…should have access to …
此外，…应该能够…
Though a multitude of achievement have been made by …,still there are some areas to be improve.
尽管…已经取得了许多成就，但有些地方仍有待改善。
Firstly,we should place more emphasis on … rather than…
首先，我们应该更注重…而不是…
