SDS

Site Unreachable

  1. 必须是居住在SDS名单中14个国家之一的合法居民；
  2. 有大专院校指定学习机构的录取通知书；
  3. 申请时居住在加拿大境外；
  4. 有证据证明你已经支付了第一年的学费；
  5. 办理了1万加元的担保投资证书(GIC)；
  6. 在您申请之前进行体检（如果需要的话）；
  7. 在您申请之前获得无犯罪证明（如果需要的话）；
  8. 有你最近的中学或中学后成绩单；
  9. 雅思(IELTS) 学术A类或一般培训G类分数为 6分或更高，单项不低于6分。

Why should I study web design and development?

  1. I remember that when I graduated from elementary school in the summer vacation, I took the first computer interest class in my life. At that time, it was still the win 95 operating system, and the main courses were still based on the DOS operating system and the simple window operation of win 95. . So since then I have laid the foundation of computer applications. Time has changed, and in a blink of an eye I became a high school English teacher. For several years in my professional career, I have always been a teacher who likes to use a computer for class, and even likes to use a computer to type instead of writing on the blackboard. An English teacher who likes to use computers the most.
  2. My own online class learning experience has taught me how important it is to build a personal website: it is always unstable for my teacher to teach online when using a third-party platform, and sometimes there are irresistible factors of the third party that lead to his lectures interrupted. Since his company established their own website , the quality of each student’s class has been guaranteed.
  3. After China’s “double reduction” policy in 2021, many English teachers in training institutions are unemployed. Because I am a high school English teacher in a private school, in order to prepare for possible changes in the future. So I wanted to create a personal teaching website to increase my income and start my second career: online teaching. Before 2016, I had already started online teaching and brought myself certain economic benefits. I also had my own teaching content on the largest domestic platform Bilibili, tiktok, little red book, and WeChat video accounts. I planned to return to China after graduation in Canada to build my own website in the future.
  4. I have started to learn the basic courses of web front-end development HTML, CSS, and javascript on the Internet, so as to lay a certain foundation for enrolling in May next year.Why not study related skill in China?
  5. As we all know, this year is an era of AI explosion, and American countries have always been far ahead in this area. Going abroad to study computer technology must be the best choice. When chatGPT and other companies’ AI are in fierce competition. At that time, we can only wait and watch, because we cannot use these AI tools in China.
  6. As an English teacher and an English user (my IELTS score: 7), studying abroad can not only allow me to learn cutting-edge professional skills in web production, but also allow me to improve my English ability in a pure English environment. Spending the same amount of time pays off twice, so I must go abroad to learn this skill.Why study in Canada?
  7. Tuition is relatively cheap;
  8. I prefer countries with beautiful natural environment;
  9. There are three friends who work and live in Toronto, Calgary and Vancouver, Canada, so I will not feel lonely when I come to study.Domestic ties:
  10. Married, with a 6-year-old son, who is about to start elementary school this year, therefore I want to finish my studies as soon as possible and return to China to reunite with my family, and to help my son with his homework as soon as possible;
  11. I have lived in Ankang, a livable small city in China, for 7 years. I have a house and a car, and I have no mortgage or car loan. Both parents live very close to my home, so it is convenient to take care of them;
  12. my parents are about 65 years old and need me to take care of them;

My Education history:
My previous education was at Xi’an physical University in Xi’an China(2007 to 2011), where I received the degree of Bachelor of English literature.I pledge to follow all the rules and regulations of Canada. I would be highly obliged and grateful if you allow me to study in Canada.
